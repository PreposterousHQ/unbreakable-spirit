# Unbreakable Spirit

**Five-Season Transmedium Docuseries · Broadcasting at the Speed of Life**

> *Not a documentary about a movement. The movement, documented as it's being lived.*

The public placeholder home of Unbreakable Spirit — a five-season reality TV docuseries reimagined as cultural intervention, premiering New Jersey 2028.

## Pillar
`02-storylivingry-studio` — Unbreakable Spirit is the **broadcast medium** of the Moviement. The layer that ties every other room together for a mass audience.

## What it is
- **Format:** Five-season transmedium docuseries (~26 episodes per season, ~130 hours total)
- **Premiere target:** New Jersey, 2028
- **Broadcast partner ambition:** Netflix Fort Monmouth — the billion-dollar production complex opening on the former Army base in Bret's home county
- **Centerpiece:** "The Future Live-Aid" — recurring set piece where philanthropists pitch the Moviement (instead of the other way around). Funding mechanism AND cinematic centerpiece in one.

## The Lineage
- **2011** — *Unbreakable Spirit: Rising Above the Impossible*, NYT bestseller compiled by Lisa Nichols. Bret's chapter: "Hero's Journey in Transforming Perceived Mental Illness Into A Blessing."
- **2023** — Continued as essay
- **2028** — Becomes docuseries
- **Beyond** — Spine of a country's mental health reformation

Same name on every layer because it's the same work in successive media.

## The Other Rooms in the Moviement
Unbreakable Spirit is one medium of seven. Each lives at its own URL:

- **One Mind Show** — Theatrical medium (`PreposterousHQ/one-mind-show` → onemindshow.com) — live, embodied, immersive frame. Stand-up, sermon, spectacle, campus by campus.

- **The Hospital That Laughter Built** — Cinematic medium — feature film whose production budget builds Patch Adams' long-unfinished hospital in West Virginia.

- **Storylivingry Studio** — Mother brand (`PreposterousHQ/storylivingry-studio` → storylivingrystudio.info) — the studio that produces all of it. Making a livingry, not a weaponry.

## Status

## Why stealth
A docuseries premiering in 2028 doesn't want premature SEO indexing. The launch moment controls the narrative; the placeholder site stays quiet until production marketing is ready to go live.

## Going live (when the time comes)
1. Remove the `noindex` meta tag from `index.html`
2. Change `robots.txt` from `Disallow: /` to `Allow: /`
3. Commit + push
4. Google starts indexing within 48 hours

## Visual posture (this site is intentionally different)
The other rooms in the ecosystem dress for institutional trust — dark, gold, theatrical, prophetic. This page deliberately does not. Unbreakable Spirit is the medium that has to play in living rooms, on phones, on screens people fall asleep to. The skin is approachable. The frame is dignified. Both on purpose.
